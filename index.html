Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Does the AI sound like a robot? Will my customers know?

Modern AI voice technology is indistinguishable from a human receptionist. Your customers won't know unless you tell them — and we'll help you craft a disclosure if you want one. The AI greets callers with your business name and speaks naturally.

What happens after the 7-day free audit?

After 7 days, you'll see a full report of every call captured, every lead collected, and the estimated revenue recovered. Then you decide if you want to continue. No hard sell — the numbers speak for themselves.

Do I have to change my phone number?

No. You keep your existing business number. We use a simple call-forward setting on your phone for missed calls and after-hours calls. Takes about 60 seconds to set up and can be toggled on/off any time.

What if I'm already using an answering service?

AI is typically faster (answers in under 3 seconds vs. 30–60 seconds for a live answering service), available 24/7/365 without per-minute fees, and never has bad days. Many contractors use it as a complement or full replacement depending on call volume.

Which industries does this work for?

We focus on home service contractors: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, general contracting, pest control, landscaping, and similar trades. If customers call to request service, this works for you.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fee on any plan. The free 7-day audit is completely free — no credit card required. If you continue, you'll pay the flat monthly rate starting on day 8.
